About My Country, Nigeria

Nigeria, country located on the western coast of Africa. Nigeria has a diverse geography, with climates ranging from arid to humid equatorial. However, Nigeria’s most diverse feature is its people. It has a population of over 200 Million people with Hundreds of languages spoken in the country, including Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, Fulani, Edo, Ibibio, Tiv, and English.
The country has abundant natural resources, notably large deposits of petroleum and natural gas.
The national capital is Abuja, known as the Federal Capital Territory, which was created by decree in 1976. Lagos, the former capital, retains its standing as the country’s leading commercial and industrial city
